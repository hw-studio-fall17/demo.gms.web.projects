Dexter Holland (Vocals and Guitar), Noodles (guitar), Greg K (bass) and Pete Paranda (drums). Following a riot at a 1984 Social Distotion show, high school buddies Dexter and Greg K decided to start a band of their own in Garden Grove, California. The Offspring have now put out 8 records, preformed over 1000 shows across the globe and are known for their many hits including "Self Esteem," "Come Out And play," "The Kids Aren't Alright, "Hammerhead" and "You're Gonna Go Far, Kid."
